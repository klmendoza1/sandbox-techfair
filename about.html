About UPSITF

We make IT happen

The UP System Information Technology Foundation (UPSITF), Inc. is a non-government organization focused on the application of Information and Communication Technology for development. It partners with the UP Information Technology Development Center (UP ITDC) in providing various services such as Education Development, Research & Development, Software Engineering, and IT Project Management, among others.

Academic Tours

Academic tours provide students from all over the Philippines comprehensive information and communication technology (ICT) related seminars. Visiting students from other schools are provided with a quick course on technology topics of their choice.
