About UPSITF

We make IT happen

The UP System Information Technology Foundation is a non-government organization focused on the application of ICT for development. It partners with the UP Information Technology Development Center (UP ITDC) in providing various services such as Education Development, Research & Development, Software Engineering, and IT Project Management.
